# Hero Section Optimization

## Objective
Redesign the hero section of the landing page to achieve 30-second comprehension for HackerNews visitors. The hero must clearly communicate: what Lucineer is, what the fleet does, and why it matters.

## Requirements
1. **Clarity**: Immediate understanding of core concept (git-native repo-agents)
2. **Visual Hierarchy**: Clear typography and layout guiding eye flow
3. **Call to Action**: Clear next steps for exploration
4. **Fleet Context**: Connection to broader Lucineer ecosystem
5. **Marine Theme**: Subtle nautical elements without overdoing it
